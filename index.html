About Us

మా గురించి

Our Mission

  • Promote Natural Resource Management through water conservation
  • Coordinate with government and NGOs for vulnerable groups
  • Improve socio-economic status through livelihood promotion
  • Promote health and nutrition education for communities
  • Gender equality, women empowerment and youth development

Our Vision

మా దార్శనికత

Holistic Development

JJWES visualizes the holistic development of society through socio-economic, cultural and political empowerment with focus on women, PWD candidates and children.

Our approach includes poverty reduction through Natural Resource Management, Climate Change adoption, renewable energy, health education, and vocational training to empower marginalized communities.
