World Menstrual Hygiene Day

Celebrating health, dignity, and awareness for every woman

Unicharam PVT LTD, Sofy, Lifree collaborate with Jal Jeevan and Women Empowerment Society for the World Menstrual Hygiene Day celebration at Dattatreya (Dasari) Colony, Kamareddy, Telangana, focusing on menstrual hygiene awareness, health education, and preventive care.

100 Women participated
4–5 Days Natural process
Health Awareness & screening

Event Overview

The program was observed in Devunipalli, Kamareddy town, with active participation from local women. It highlighted the importance of menstrual hygiene, infection prevention, and safe healthcare practices.

Key Focus

  • Menstrual hygiene awareness
  • Health precautions during menstruation
  • Medical guidance from experts
  • Community participation and screening

About The Event

A special awareness program was organized to educate women about menstrual hygiene and the importance of maintaining health and dignity during menstruation.

Community Awareness

The event brought together local women, government hospital staff, and community leaders to create awareness about healthy menstrual practices and the risks of neglecting hygiene.

Health Education

Speakers shared guidance on infection prevention, menstrual care, and the importance of safe health habits. Blood tests were also conducted as part of the awareness initiative.

Program Highlights

Key moments and outcomes from the celebration.

100 Women Participated

Local women actively joined the event and participated in the awareness sessions.

Expert Guidance

Health professionals and school leaders shared practical advice on hygiene and safety.

Health Screening

Blood tests and related health checks were conducted during the awareness program.
